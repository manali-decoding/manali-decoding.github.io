Hi, I’m Manali.

a Web Developer & UX Designer

I'm a Web Developer and a UX Designer based in Seattle, Washington.

Whether it is UX design or programming a new application, I have a creative side that I have always enjoyed exploring and growing. Whether I’m working on low-fidelity wireframes or building high-quality web UI, I find ways to empathize with users through research and in-depth understanding of the problem at hand. As a UX designer, I understand user experience best practices and design fundamentals to make strategically sound decisions. This makes me a right fit to build interfaces with a deep understanding of how people interact with them. User’s experience is very important to me, so I strive to craft an interface that is innovative, visually appealing, and intuitive for everyone.
